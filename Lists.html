The Best Pot Roast Ever

Follow these directions to make the best pot roast you have ever had.

Ingredients

  • 3-5lb Chuck Roast
  • 1 packet of Ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 packet of Italian seasoning mix
  • 1 packet of Brown gravy mix
  • 2 cups of water

Directions

  1. Place roast in the crock pot
  2. Add 2 cups of water
  3. Pour all of the seasoning packets on top of the roast
  4. Cover crock pot with the lid
  5. Set crock pot on low, and cook for 10 hours
